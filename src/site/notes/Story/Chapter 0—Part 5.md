## We're Working On It, Folks!
In [[Locations/Popolala\|Popolala]], specifically in the small northern town of Fluffycloud Meadows. This is where [[Characters/Popolala/Sailor Sprinklepopglittercool\|Sailor Sprinklepopglittercool]] has been living incognito for the last several months. Going by the name Trudy, she has been in disguise with huge glasses and baggy clothes. The town is populated with stuffles who are unaware of her nature as a magical girl, let alone being the champion. The only one who knows is her companion [[Characters/Popolala/Cotton Blue\|Cotton Blue]].

Due to recent incidents, she is no longer concerned with her magical destiny. She's started living a comfortable and (mostly) solitary life. She wants to be left alone by the residents, but she does enjoy baking, and the stuffles love her pies. She might even be slowly making friends, so long as they continue to praise her baking efforts. But it's not quite fast enough friend-making by Cotton Blue's standards.

This afternoon, Trudy has shut herself up in her house to get away from the residents, who have been singing happy songs nonstop for the past few hours. Cotton Blue tries unsuccessfully to convince her to enjoy it. Then suddenly, they are interrupted by a thump against the window. She picks up a rolling pin to "defend" herself and faces the window - pressed up against it is her eagle-like stuffle neighbor [[Characters/Popolala/Minor Popolalans\|Fizzlepip]].

Eventually, Trudy reluctantly lets him into the house. He announces the reason for his visit - he'd like one of Trudy's famous Peachy Poppy Pies. She's flattered, but also wants him to go away, so agrees to make him a pie if he leaves. He tells her that will be okay, since he will be taking the pie to the party to share with all his friends. He also offers her a large emerald as payment.

Trudy is unable to determine how valuable such a thing would be in this world, but she accepts nonetheless. Fizzlepip then invites them to join him at the party. Eventually she gives in, looking forward to the praise and payments she will receive if she brings more pies.

She and Cotton Blue spend the next few hours preparing more pies, and then bring them all to the party on a pushcart. All of the stuffles in attendance are overjoyed, and she is paid with a massive diamond. Fizzlepip indicates that the whole reason for this party is that they are hosting a very special guest, and he's surprised they don't know about it.

Once pie slices are passed out to everyone, another group of guests arrive - it's [[Characters/Popolala/Minor Popolalans\|PopoPrincess Threadspin]], the princess of Popolala, and her entourage. This includes [[Characters/Popolala/Minor Popolalans\|Pretty HeartCaptor Lilax]], an up-and-coming magical girl meant to take Trudy's place. The gravity of the situation begins to dawn on Cotton Blue, and he realizes they need to make a quick escape.

While Trudy and Cotton Blue whisper to each other, Fizzlepip tells the new guests about all the pies that have been provided by Trudy. Lilax seems keen on keeping everyone's attention on her. She introduces herself to everyone as the champion, and also reveals that she brought her own "superior" Peachy Poppy Pie.

Enraged, Trudy has an outburst about Lilax's attitude. This unfortunately causes the PopoPrincess to realize who she is, and demands that she remove her disguise. She also turns her anger on Cotton Blue for continuing to aid Trudy. As Lilax shows herself to be more and more disrespectful of the pair, Trudy escalates this into a fight. They take it outside.

The battle gets more and more violent, despite the attempts of Trudy and Lilax's companions to de-escalate the situation. Trudy refuses to back down because of the grudge she's holding.  Finally, Cotton Blue casts a spell to calm everyone down. It succeeds - on everyone except Trudy.

Trudy takes this opportunity to demand apologies from Lilax and the PopoPrincess. They both offer half-hearted ones. Lilax is convinced to try a slice of Trudy's pie, and admits that it's very good. The PopoPrincess chooses her words carefully to satisfy Trudy without actually pardoning her crimes.

Everything seems calm, but Trudy can't resist throwing a pie in Lilax's face as she leaves. Then she bolts out of town with Cotton Blue following close behind.
