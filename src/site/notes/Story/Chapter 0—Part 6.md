## Folks Don't Appre-Sea-ate My Puns!
It is early afternoon in Adomete, a prominent land-based town in [[Locations/Melusio\|Melusio]]. As she often does, [[Characters/Melusio/Mirina\|Mirina]] has come here today to visit with [[Characters/Melusio/Sid Storm\|Sid]]. However, when she arrives at his house, the door is standing open somewhat ominously.

Not seeing anyone inside, she decides to write him a note and leave it inside. She turns to leave, then bumps into an undine man who entered the house after her. She recognizes his flashy outfit as being the ceremonial uniform of the royal guard of Melusio.

The man, [[Characters/Melusio/Minor Melusians\|Captain Kelleis]], begins interrogating her as to why she is here. Upon realizing that she considers herself to be a friend to Sid, he is happy to inform her that she doesn't have to worry about him anymore. The captain has already “helped” Sid on his way out of town.

Mirina keeps asking him where Sid is, but he is being deliberately vague about the details. Eventually, he realizes that he recognizes her as the undine who often accompanies the so-called champion on his misadventures. He comes to the conclusion that she must feel some sort of obligation to aid and protect Sid, and that now she is free of him. She protests, but he is convinced of his viewpoint.

Finally, he explains to her that he and his subordinates dragged Sid out of the city. He also believes that the best plan of action is to keep her in Adomete and prevent her from going after Sid. He offers to bring her back home to her family, but she insists that she has been on the road for a long time now and is self-sufficient.

He brings her outside, where she encounters two of his subordinates who have been waiting here the whole time. She is unable to convince any of them that she is helping Sid of her own free will. To her continued horror, she learns that the whole reason the knights are here today is to dispose of all of Sid's belongings, emptying the house. Sid has officially been banished from Melusio. They fought Sid on the outskirts of Adomete, and left him there after Kelleis knocked him out.

She hurries in to stop them. The two knights become frustrated with her efforts, but Kelleis has become sympathetic towards her. One of the subordinates proposes that the captain grant her wish to take her to where Sid was last seen, so they can get on with their job - Kelleis reluctantly agrees. But at the last moment, Mirina claims that some of her own belongings are mixed in with Sid's. Kelleis decides to postpone the disposal order to a later date.

Mirina and Kelleis head to the edge of the city, and the closer they get, the more signs of battle they come across. The most telling sign is scorch marks on the stone walls. But even once they are outside the city limits, there is still no sign of Sid. He tells her that Sid could have gone anywhere, but that he likely hasn't returned to the city. There is nothing else he can do, so he simply wishes her well on her journey.

She ventures out into the wilderness a ways, but still can't seem to find him. Becoming desperate, she cries out to him as loud as she can. Sid is able to hear her, and emerges from the trees. He is shocked that she followed him, but allows her to stick around. And so they wander off, anywhere but Melusio.
