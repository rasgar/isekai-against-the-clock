## Keep Those Folks Away From Me
Afternoon on a clear, sunny day in Erranthemar, the capital city of [[Locations/Seleneas\|Seleneas]]. [[Characters/Seleneas/Razia Celestia\|Razia Celestia]] is on her way to the castle gardens for some meditation. Alas, someone is waiting there for her who has clearly been looking for her -  her peace and solitude will have to wait.

Razia can't be bothered to remember who this person is at the moment, but it is [[Characters/Seleneas/Minor Seleneans\|Eluniore]], a maid in the employ of the royal family. Months ago, she was selected by the Queen herself to be Razia's personal handmaiden. And she is here today to bring Razia a summons from the King and Queen.

Reluctantly, Razia skips her meditation and heads towards the throne room. Along the way, she catches numerous castle servants watching her. "It's the Champion!" they say, whispering reverently. At the top of the staircase leading to the throne room's doors is [[Characters/Seleneas/Taegen Estellia\|Taegen Estellia]]. He is the prince of Seleneas and Razia's fiancé - though not by their choosing.

Before she can address him, one of the servants approaches her and asks if the rumors are true, that Razia is a physical avatar of [[Characters/Seleneas/Minor Seleneans\|Nodia]]. Razia denies this, stating that she was simply sent by the Celestial Twins - the primary deities worshipped in Seleneas, Nodia the moon goddess and her brother [[Characters/Seleneas/Minor Seleneans\|Lumir]] the god of the stars.

This leads to a few servants eagerly debating rumors and theories with her, including that she might be a child of one of the deities, or a previously unknown third sibling who is the goddess of the sun. She continues to deny all these theories, but can't resist smugly agreeing that she is more powerful than the royal family.

With the conversation dragging on, Taegen comes down the stairs and confronts her about how late she is to this important meeting. Both of their moods sour when they realize that the group is watching them and commenting on how good the "royal couple" looks together. Razia blames the servants for holding her up, causing them to back off and apologize. The pair finally enters the throne room.

Sitting here as usual are [[Characters/Seleneas/Minor Seleneans\|King Tessaryn]] and [[Characters/Seleneas/Minor Seleneans\|Queen Almeda]]. Much to Razia's dismay, they want hugs before they can start talking. In fact, she accidentally swaps their names as a result of being grumpy. Taegen covers for her, explaining that she's out of sorts since she missed her morning meditation.

King Tessaryn explains the reason why they've called them here, referencing the fact that at one time, there were sea monsters cutting off Selenean sailing routes. Razia helped them by destroying these monsters with her powers. In the process, they learned that sea monsters are much tastier than the usual fisherman hauls.

Today, they would like for Razia to go out and hunt a sea monster to replenish their stock of ingredients. A sailor recently sighted a giant crustacean monster called a chuul near the island's shore, making this a matter of safety as well as excellent cuisine. Furthermore, he and Almeda would like for Taegen to accompany her to gain combat experience (and a bonding opportunity).

The two of them agree to the task somewhat reluctantly. As Taegen heads down to the docks to prepare a boat for them, he crosses paths with Eluniore, who wishes him luck in dealing with Razia. They share a knowing look before he departs.

Soon, Razia and Taegen are sailing out towards the last sighting of the chuul. It is a mostly uneventful trip, with the two of them avoiding speaking to each other. Taegen eventually sees ripples in the water, and they begin to prepare for battle. He is able to maneuver the boat just in time and narrowly avoid it getting crushed by the chuul's claws. Once it surfaces, the pair manages to defeat the monster using their combined magical powers, making a surprisingly good team. However, the boat is destroyed in the process. Taegen reluctantly allows Razia to tow him back to the island while she floats over the water's surface.

A crowd is there to welcome them back, and fishermen reel in the chuul's remains. The queen offers Taegen a blanket since he's soaked. However, the king notices something disturbing - Razia has several splinters of wood from the boat embedded in her, and she is bleeding. Taegen quickly throws the blanket over her to prevent the crowd from seeing the damage.

The king and queen bring them back to the castle so they can talk in private. Razia is stunned at this turn of events, and Almeda fears that Nodia has abandoned them. The king feels they should consult the clergy in Erranthemar to divine what the Twins intend by this. Taegen points out that they should tell as few people as possible to avoid creating a panic. Ultimately, they agree to send Razia and Taegen to [[Locations/Cepriso\|Cepriso]] incognito, so that they can seek information without raising any suspicions.
