**Ryunosuke Swordguy** (RYE-yoo-no-SOO-kay)
A human who refers to himself as "the Champion", even when learning that there seems to be more than one. He comes from the same world as the other champions. Seems to have traveled from the country of Minamatsu alongside Temuldrak the Grimbo. According to Temuldrak, Ryunosuke is a self-described otaku. He aims to "invent" manga in Ritravis.

**Temuldrak the Grimbo**
A large, green-skinned man who travels with Ryunosuke. He is a man of few words. He's not particularly fond of the so-called Champion of Minamatsu, but seems duty-bound to follow him.

**Blaque**
A dwarven blacksmith who runs a successful business in [[Locations/Cepriso\|Odonphis]]. He is experienced in the maintenance of magically enchanted equipment. Lately, it seems like he is considering plying his trade in New Fabergar.

**Adria**
A young woman studying for some unspecified goal, met in the library in [[Locations/Cepriso\|Odonphis]]. She is very shy and quiet. She fell in love with the disguised [[Characters/Seleneas/Taegen Estellia\|Taegen]] at first sight, became jealous of [[Characters/Seleneas/Razia Celestia\|Razia]], and was then comforted by [[Characters/Popolala/Cotton Blue\|Cotton Blue]]. She wants to believe his advice that she can be a "princess" by being herself, and that it will let her find her "prince".

**Mysterious Green**
Quite mysterious.

**Duralle**
A desk clerk working at an inn in [[Locations/Cepriso\|Odonphis]]. She's proud of the cinnamon rolls she bakes for the inn's customers.
