**Fizzlepip**
An eagle-like stuffle who lived near [[Characters/Popolala/Sailor Sprinklepopglittercool\|Trudy]] in Fluffycloud Meadows, and a friend to [[Characters/Popolala/Cotton Blue\|Cotton Blue]].

**Blankie Gray**
A horse-like stuffle who attended Fizzlepip's party. He loves apples and apple pie. He walks on all fours.

**PopoPrincess Threadspin**
A pink chipmunk-like stuffle with hedgehog spines going down her back. She is a bit larger than the average stuffle, and has huge adorable eyes. Looking directly at her probably gives you diabetes. She is the princess of Popolala, and wants [[Characters/Popolala/Sailor Sprinklepopglittercool\|Sailor Sprinklepopglittercool]] arrested.

**Pretty HeartCaptor Lilax**
A human girl who is quite new to magical girl-ing. She was recruited to fill the void left by [[Characters/Popolala/Sailor Sprinklepopglittercool\|Sailor Sprinklepopglittercool]]. Lovely, friendly and elegant, she is everything a magical girl ought to be, and everything Trudy is not. She does, however, seem to have an ego problem. Her weapon is a big heart-shaped pillow.

**Satin Yellow**
A seahorse-like stuffle with a cat-like head. She is the stuffle guardian of Lilax, who typically hangs over her magical girl's shoulders like a boa, and she can also be wielded as a whip. She has a much more timid personality than her companion.
