**Captain Kelleis** (kell-ACE)
A captain  in the royal guard of Melusio. He was responsible for ejecting [[Characters/Melusio/Sid Storm\|Sid]] from [[Locations/Melusio\|Adomete]] due to the orders he received. He is concerned for [[Characters/Melusio/Mirina\|Mirina]]'s well-being, but nevertheless assisted her in finding Sid so she could follow him.

**Terephi** (TARE-uh-fee)
An undine child who carries a plush dolphin named Fluffy. She is [[Characters/Melusio/Mirina\|Mirina]]'s younger sister, who Mirina had not seen since she went off to school before meeting [[Characters/Melusio/Sid Storm\|Sid]]. She currently resides in [[Locations/Cepriso\|Odonphis]] with her parents, who are busy with the Cultural Exchange Center.
