**The Prince**
A mean guy who doesn't like [[Characters/Brugatiel/Rodrique Khil\|Rod]]. According to many people in the nation, he and Rod look strikingly similar. Rod doesn't remember his name.

**Rena**
An elderly gnome tailor who works in [[Locations/Brugatiel\|Rominter]]. She made [[Characters/Brugatiel/Brank O. Fjord\|Brank]]'s suit shortly after his arrival.

**Rugged Ebony**
A dog-like stuffle who moved out of [[Locations/Popolala\|Popolala]] with a heavy heart, due to his severe allergy to rainbows. Despite his origins, he has a rough look about him - including an eyepatch and cigar - befitting a citizen of Brugatiel. Among his battle scars was a missing "eye". He traded his eyepatch to [[Characters/Brugatiel/Rodrique Khil\|Rod]] for a plastic ruby-like button, which [[Characters/Brugatiel/Brank O. Fjord\|Brank]] sewed onto his face. He was struggling to make a life as an adventurer, but Rod (accidentally) opened him up to the possibility of becoming an artist.
